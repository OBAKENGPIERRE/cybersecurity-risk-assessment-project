# Cybersecurity Risk Heat Map

This heat map categorizes risks based on likelihood and impact.

| Impact \ Likelihood | Low | Medium | High |
|---|---|---|---|
| High | Medium | High | Critical |
| Medium | Low | Medium | High |
| Low | Low | Low | Medium |

## Risk Levels

Low Risk  
Minor security impact with low likelihood.

Medium Risk  
Moderate risk requiring monitoring.

High Risk  
Serious security concern requiring mitigation.

Critical Risk  
Immediate action required.
