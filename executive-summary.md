# Executive Security Summary

## Overview

This report summarises the results of a cybersecurity risk assessment conducted
for a simulated small business IT environment. The purpose of the assessment
was to identify potential cybersecurity risks affecting the organisation’s
systems, data, and operations.

The assessment evaluated critical assets, potential threats, existing
vulnerabilities, and the overall risk exposure of the organisation.

---

## Key Findings

The assessment identified several significant cybersecurity risks that could
impact the organisation’s operations and data security.

The most notable risks include:

- Phishing attacks targeting employee credentials
- Malware infections on employee workstations
- Unauthorized access to sensitive company files
- Weak password policies
- Lack of multi-factor authentication

These risks could lead to data breaches, service disruptions, and potential
financial or reputational damage.

---

## Risk Severity Overview

| Risk Level | Description |
|---|---|
| Critical | Immediate action required |
| High | Significant risk requiring mitigation |
| Medium | Moderate risk requiring monitoring |
| Low | Minor risk with minimal impact |

The assessment identified multiple **High and Critical risks** that should be
prioritised for remediation.

---

## Recommended Actions

The following actions are recommended to improve the organisation's security
posture:

- Implement multi-factor authentication (MFA)
- Conduct regular employee security awareness training
- Deploy endpoint protection solutions
- Enforce strong password policies
- Conduct regular vulnerability assessments
- Maintain regular software patching and updates

---

## Conclusion

By implementing the recommended security controls and following recognised
cybersecurity frameworks, the organisation can significantly reduce its
exposure to cyber threats and strengthen its overall security posture.

Continuous monitoring, risk assessments, and security awareness initiatives
are essential to maintaining a strong cybersecurity program.
